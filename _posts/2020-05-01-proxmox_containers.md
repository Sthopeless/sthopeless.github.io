<A href="#section-1">Proxmox</A>

My collection of Proxmox containers installers, they are heavy inspired on [whiskerz007](https://github.com/whiskerz007/proxmox_portainer_lxc).
Containers are using Debian some with and some without Docker

Repositories can be found either on my [Github](https://github.com/Sthopeless?tab=repositories) or on my [Gitea](https://git.sthopeless.com/sthope)

Some may not yet be working or need extra configuration, with time I will try to sort them out and document it on their page.
